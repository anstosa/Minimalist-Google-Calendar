Google Calendar | import/export

Import/Export

To transfer settings, click "Export", copy everything, paste it back in another browser and click "Import".

Presets

Warning: clicking a preset will replace your current settings. Your current settings will be saved to "Custom 3", overriding it's contents.



Full Reset

If you are having problems and just want to start fresh, use this button. It will clear all local settings and reset them to Google Calendar's default.

Credits

My name is Ansel Santosa [@anstosa]. I'm an Informatics student at the University of Washington in Seattle studying to to a UX Designer.

I created Minimalist for Gmail because none of the others on the market would cut it for me. It was just a handful of personal scripts for a while. But I recently decided to polish and publish.

Minimalist for Google Calendar is just the second in what I hope will become a suite of Google webapp customizers
